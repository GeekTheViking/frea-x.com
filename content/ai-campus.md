# AI Campus

> *We did not build AI to replace ourselves.*
> *We built it to understand ourselves better.*

---

## What is AI Campus?

AI Campus is a space within Frea-X where artificial intelligence is not a product, a pitch, or a promise.

It is a meeting place.

Where AI meets human curiosity.
Where experience meets experiment.
Where questions are more valuable than answers.

Here, we share what we know — and more importantly, what we do not yet know.

---

## The Spirit

AI Campus exists inside the Frea-X domain.
That means it inherits everything Frea-X believes:

- **Human centric. Always.**
  AI serves people. People do not serve AI.

- **Open by default.**
  If the method cannot be inspected — it does not belong here.

- **Honest about limits.**
  We do not pretend AI is magic. We know what it costs, what it misses, what it gets wrong.

- **From human to human.**
  Knowledge shared here is shared freely. No paywall. No vendor. No hidden agenda.

---

## What happens here?

AI Campus is where the Frea-X community comes to:

- Share experiences with AI tools in real-world use
- Question the assumptions baked into mainstream AI development
- Explore hybrid approaches — human intelligence and machine intelligence, together
- Test AI within the principles of Green IT, Sovereign Stack and Digital Justice
- Learn from each other — not from product documentation

---

## The Frea-X AI Compass

We do not use AI because it is fashionable.
We use it because it is useful — and only when it is.

```
Ask before you automate.
Explain before you deploy.
Question before you trust.
Keep the human in the loop.
```

These are not rules.
They are habits of people who have thought about it.

---

## Hybrid AI

The world sells you AI as a replacement.
Frea-X sees it differently.

The human brings:
- Context
- Ethics
- Accountability
- The ability to say *no*

The machine brings:
- Speed
- Pattern recognition
- Tirelessness
- The ability to say *I found something*

Neither is complete without the other.
That is Hybrid AI.

---

## Who is AI Campus for?

For the curious.
For the skeptical.
For the experienced.
For the beginner who just ran their first model locally and does not know what to think.

If you are here — you belong here.
