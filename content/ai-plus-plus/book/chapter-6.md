# Chapter 6 — PROSTEIN

## The Vision

> *Fire stolen from the gods.*
> *Life breathed into the parts.*
> *Now yours to keep.*
>
> *From human to human.*
> *From all of us, to all of you.*
>
> *PROSTEIN — Yours forever.*

---

## What it is

PROSTEIN is not the biggest AI.
Not the most funded.
Not the one with the best benchmark score.

PROSTEIN is the one that shows up for the people the benchmarks forgot.

---

## Who it is for

For the person who cannot read the letter from the municipality.
For the elderly who needs to know what this medication interacts with.
For the newcomer navigating an asylum application alone at midnight.
For the student who cannot afford a tutor.
For the small business owner drowning in forms they did not design.
For anyone who has ever felt that technology was built for someone else.

**PROSTEIN was built for you.**

Not as marketing language.
As a constraint that was written down before any code was written.

---

## The questions it answers

```
Read this letter aloud — I cannot see well.
What does this medication do?
Help me understand my tax return.
What are my rights in this situation?
Translate this for me.
I am lonely. Can we talk?
What is happening in my city today?
```

Not world-changing questions.
Everyday questions.

The questions that deserve an answer
regardless of who is asking,
regardless of what they can pay.

---

## The Economy

PROSTEIN runs on:

- Tax-funded public infrastructure where it exists
- Voluntary contributions from individuals who believe
- Donated compute cycles from organisations with capacity to spare
- Hardware rescued from obsolescence
- Time given freely by people who could charge more elsewhere

It is accountable to its users — not to investors.
Because it has no investors.
It has **believers.**

This is not idealism.
It is a funding model that has worked before.
Libraries. Cooperative dairies. Open-source software.
The pattern is old. The application is new.

---

## The Boundary

PROSTEIN will not be weaponised.

Not for targeted manipulation.
Not for precision surveillance.
Not for harm of any kind — however it is framed.

We cannot control everything.
We know this.
Humans can be unutterably cruel to one another.

But we will not build the tools for it.
And we will not stay silent when we see others doing so.

> *Be the signal, not the noise.*

---

## The Relationship to AI++

PROSTEIN is not AI++.
AI++ is not PROSTEIN.

PROSTEIN is the destination — the vision of AI that belongs to people.
AI++ is the working method that gets there.

You practice AI++ today, with the tools you have.
You build toward PROSTEIN — the infrastructure that does not depend on anyone's generosity to survive.

One is the journey. The other is the destination.

Both matter. Neither is enough without the other.
