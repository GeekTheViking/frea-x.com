# Quality Control

<p class="fx-mantra">THINK BEFORE YOU ACT.</p>
<p class="fx-mantra">THINK OF OTHERS.</p>

*Then it will never become a problem.*

---

## The Rules — Simple. No Exceptions.

### 1. If you do not understand it — do not deploy it.
- You are responsible for what you put into CyberSpace.
- "The AI did it" is not an answer.
- If you cannot explain it, you cannot control it.

### 2. Children are new citizens of CyberSpace.
- They did not choose to be here.
- They do not yet know the rules.
- Protect them. Do not exploit that.

### 3. Ignorance is not an invitation.
- Someone who does not know — deserves to learn. Not to be taken advantage of.
- The clever have a responsibility to the less informed. Not a privilege over them.

### 4. Minorities must be heard.
- CyberSpace belongs to everyone.
- Majority volume does not equal majority truth.
- Make room.

### 5. Freedom requires maintenance.
- Closed systems, censored networks and gatekept information do not maintain themselves.
- Support the infrastructure of freedom: open protocols, encrypted communication, neutral networks.
- If a journalist in a closed country cannot speak — CyberSpace has failed.

### 6. What you build — you own.
- The consequences of your code, your content, your AI — they follow you.
- Build as if your name is on it. Because it is.

---

*Quality control in CyberSpace is not a checklist.*
*It is a habit. Applied every time. By everyone. Including AI.*
