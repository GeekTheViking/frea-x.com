### Why Authentik?

Some asked: *"But isn't Authentik an American company?"*

Yes. Authentik Security Inc. is based in San Francisco.

And that is beside the point.

CyberSpace has no borders. It reaches past Venus and Mercury. The Frea-X principle is not *where* code was written — it is whether the code is open, auditable, and under your control. Authentik is MIT-licensed open source. You run it on your own servers. Your identity data never leaves your infrastructure. No telemetry. No vendor lock-in.

To reject good open source software based on the nationality of the company behind it would be to build exactly the kind of wall we are trying to tear down. That is not sovereignty — that is a smaller cage.

**We judge software by what it does in your hands. Not by what flag flies above its creators.**

Authentik earns its place on this list.
