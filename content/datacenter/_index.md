# The Frea-X Datacenter Standard
*Regeneration. Sovereignty. Symbiosis.*

![Frea-X Certified](/images/frea-x-badge.png)

Datacenters are the backbone of the digital age — yet today most are extractive "islands": consuming power, water and land while giving little back to the people who live beside them. **Frea-X proposes the opposite.**

Under the Frea-X standard, a datacenter becomes a **Resource Bank** and an **Energy Anchor** — a living organ in its local ecosystem. It sequesters carbon in its very structure, stabilises the regional power grid, cools itself with the earth instead of drinking water, reuses its own hardware to the last drop, and hands real prosperity back to the community that hosts it.
