# The Shield Checklist
*For citizens & communities — the questions to demand of any datacenter project.*

When a datacenter is proposed near you, you don't have to choose between *"yes to anything"* and *"no to everything."* You can demand it be done **right**.

Bring this checklist to your municipality, your local debate, or the developer's open meeting. A **Frea-X Shield-aligned** project should be able to answer **yes** to these. If it can't — ask why.

## 💧 Water
- ☐ **Zero drinking water** used for cooling?
- ☐ Cooling via **earth-tubes, free-air and rainwater harvesting** (closed loop)?

## ♨️ Energy & Heat
- ☐ **100% of surplus heat** fed back into district heating (or local food production)?
- ☐ Does the project **stabilise the local grid** (e.g. sand batteries, Power-to-X)?
- ☐ For mega-projects: is **at least 60% of the power reserved for the local population**? *(the 60/40 Sovereignty Split)*

## 🏗️ Materials & Hardware
- ☐ Built from **mass timber (CLT)** and sequestered waste — not pure concrete?
- ☐ **Hardware reused and repaired**, not disposable? E-waste recovered, never landfilled?

## 🌐 Digital & Community
- ☐ **Local fiber "exits"** so the community that hosts the data also gets the speed?
- ☐ **Data sovereignty** — local/EU control, open standards?
- ☐ **Local jobs & training** (a real skills/AI-campus commitment)?

## 🌱 Nature
- ☐ **Dark-sky lighting**, biodiversity net-gain, and subterranean/noise control?

## 🛡️ Accountability
- ☐ A **Frea-X People's Audit** — by local citizens *and* independent experts?
- ☐ A **revocable "License to Operate"** if the project fails to deliver?
- ☐ Will it earn the **Frea-X Shield** (🔵 Blue · 🟢 Green · 🟡 Gold)?

---

**The bottom line:** say no to "Black" datacenters that merely *"do less harm."* Demand one that makes your community **better**.
