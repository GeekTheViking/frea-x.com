# Frea-X Global Benchmark: Infrastructure Scorecard
*Measuring digital giants against regenerative standards.*

This scorecard is the Frea-X tool for local communities and governments to evaluate whether a proposed infrastructure project deserves a **"License to Operate."** We use current projects as case studies to show the gap between *"Business as Usual"* and the **Frea-X Gold Standard.**

## Case Study: Faxe Project (Denmark)
*Status: Proposal Phase · Estimated Investment: 20–30 Billion DKK*

| Feature | Current Industry Standard (Faxe Proposal) | Frea-X Gold Requirement | Score |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Construction Materials** | Conventional concrete & steel | Mass Timber (CLT), recycled plastics, ash-concrete | 🔴 Fail |
| **Water Consumption** | Ground water / evaporative cooling | Zero-water earth-tubes & geothermal loops | 🔴 Fail |
| **Aesthetics & Landscape** | Large industrial "boxes" above ground | Subterranean integration & living algae facades | 🔴 Fail |
| **Energy Role** | Massive net consumer from grid | Regional Energy Bank (sand batteries & PtX) | 🔴 Fail |
| **Heat Recovery** | Optional / secondary | 100% mandatory district-heating integration | 🟡 Partial |
| **Biodiversity** | Fenced grass & asphalt deserts | Wild habitats, rainwater catchment, dark sky | 🔴 Fail |
| **Connectivity** | High-speed bypass (no local access) | Guaranteed fiber "exits" for local villages | 🔴 Fail |
| **Oversight** | Corporate self-reporting | **The People's Audit** (citizen-led governance) | 🔴 Fail |

## How to use the Scorecard
If a project scores more than three **🔴 red marks**, it is an **Extractive Project** — it drains local resources and offers no systemic value to the future.

### The Frea-X Grading System
- **Grade A — 🟡 Gold Shield:** meets all 8 criteria. A true "Resource Bank."
- **Grade B — 🟢 Green Shield:** meets at least 6, including Water and Materials.
- **Grade C — 🔵 Blue Shield:** meets at least 4, focusing on Sovereignty and Connectivity.
- **Grade F — ⬛ No Shield:** fails more than 4 criteria. **License to Operate denied.**
