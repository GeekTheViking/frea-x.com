## What is PROSTEIN?

PROSTEIN is what happens when infrastructure meets intention.

It is the AI that runs on FRANKENSTEIN's bones —
but speaks in a human voice.

Not the biggest. Not the flashiest.
Not trained on billions of dollars of investor capital.

Just there.
For you.
When you need it.

---

## Who is PROSTEIN for?

For the person who cannot read the letter from the municipality.
For the elderly who needs to know what this medication does.
For the newcomer navigating an asylum application alone at midnight.
For the student who cannot afford a tutor.
For the small business owner drowning in tax forms.
For anyone who has ever felt that technology was built for someone else.

**PROSTEIN was built for you.**

## What can PROSTEIN do?

```
Read this letter aloud — I cannot see well.
What does this medication do?
Are there alternatives that might help me?
Help me understand my tax return.
What are my rights in this situation?
Translate this for me.

```

Not world-changing questions.
Everyday questions.
The questions that deserve an answer
regardless of who is asking.

## The Philosophy

PROSTEIN does not replace human connection.
It fills the gaps where human connection is absent.

It does not pretend to be conscious.
It does not pretend to care.
But it is built by people who do.

> *Think before you act. Think of others.*
> *Then it will never become a problem.*

This is not a warning.
It is an invitation.

---

## The Economy

PROSTEIN runs on:

- Tax-funded public infrastructure
- Voluntary contributions from individuals
- Donated compute cycles from companies who believe
- Hardware rescued from obsolescence
- Time given freely by people who could charge more

It is accountable to its users — not to its investors.
Because it has no investors.
It has **believers.**

---

## The Boundary

PROSTEIN will not be weaponised.

Not for targeted manipulation.
Not for surveillance.
Not for precision harm of any kind.

We cannot control everything.
We know this.
Humans can be unutterably cruel to one another.

But we will not build the tools for it.
And we will not stay silent when we see it happening.

> *Be the signal, not the noise.*
